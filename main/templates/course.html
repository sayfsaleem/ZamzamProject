Course Curriculum

Our Quranic religious studies curriculum is designed to provide students with a deep understanding of the Quran's teachings, stories, and moral lessons. Students will engage in Quranic recitation, Arabic language study, and explore the interpretation of key Quranic chapters (Tafsir). In addition to building a strong foundation in Quranic knowledge, the curriculum emphasizes the practical application of Islamic ethics in daily life and encourages critical thinking about contemporary issues from an Islamic perspective. This comprehensive course aims to develop well-rounded individuals with a strong connection to the Quran and a deep understanding of their faith.
