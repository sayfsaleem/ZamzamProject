Frequently Asked Questions

There is no age limit for taking classes. All ages are welcome to register for the classes or courses they want to take.

It all depends on how much the students can handle. We have recommended days and duration for each course. Of course, it can be reduced or even increased.

Just click on the Enroll button and you will be redirected to a form, in which you can fill out details and then you will shortly be contacted by an agent.

If you wish to change/drop classes, you are most welcome to get in contact with the admin assigned to you and let them know at least a week beforehand in order to save any inconveniences.

The only technical requirements that are required is the know-how to use Zoom Application.

Contact Us at zamzamacademy2023@gmail.com or +971-58-589-4795. Only call.

The teacher will be providing you with all the standard materials needed for class. Other than that you can head to our downloads section, and download the materials as needed.

Head over to our Courses page where there is a list of courses and their fee mentioned.

Payment for classes is paid in advance, but can be catered to the needs of the student.

We are currently accepting payment by credit/debit card, PayPal, and direct deposit.
